From first contact to strategic next steps — with team ownership at every stage

Based on the interest and engagement of an organization, the first step is to gauge whether they have the capacity and willingness to go deeper on a partnership that advances one or more of the Hub's strategic priorities in content, educator engagement & implementation, or marketing & outreach. Ownership of each relationship is determined by partner type and depth of engagement.

Who owns first contact?

While the first point of contact may come from various members on TAAF/FPC teams based on pre-existing relationships, next steps should be passed to the following point people based on ownership area:.

  • Terry: Existing grantees or organizations who may be potential grantees based on other TAAF education priorities, and conference/marketing partners
  • Melody: Content-only partners whose materials will be translated onto the Hub
  • Tom: Potential PD implementation, educator network, or state toolkit partners
  • Rakhee/Lena: Hub Heroes or partners who can provide testimonials or impact statements
